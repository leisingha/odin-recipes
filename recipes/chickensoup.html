Chicken Soup

Description

Chicken soup is a comforting, warm soup made with chicken, vegetables, and flavorful broth. It’s known for its soothing qualities, often used as a remedy for colds or chilly weather. The classic chicken soup recipe includes ingredients like chicken, carrots, celery, onions, and herbs for a flavorful and nourishing dish.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast or thighs
  • Carrots
  • Celery
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Chicken broth
  • Salt and pepper
  • Thyme and parsley

Steps

You’ll find the full, step-by-step recipe below — but here’s a brief overview of what you can expect when you make homemade chicken soup: Start by sautéing the onion, garlic, carrots, and celery. Add the chicken and broth, then season with salt, pepper, and herbs. Simmer until the chicken is tender and the flavors are well combined. Shred the chicken, then return it to the pot and cook for a few more minutes. Serve warm with optional garnish like fresh parsley.
