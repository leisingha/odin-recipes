Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a wide, flat sheet of pasta. Lasagna can refer to either the type of noodle or to the typical lasagna dish which is a dish made with several layers of lasagna sheets with sauce and other ingredients, such as meats and cheese, in between the lasagna noodles.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Egg
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Minced garlic
  • Tomato paste
  • Oregano

Steps

You’ll find the full, step-by-step recipe below — but here’s a brief overview of what you can expect when you make homemade easy lasagna: Cook and drain the ground beef, then stir in the spaghetti sauce and simmer. Combine the cottage cheese, 2 cups of mozzarella, eggs, half of the Parmesan, and seasonings. Assemble the lasagna according to the detailed recipe. Bake, covered, for 45 minutes. Uncover and continue baking for 10 minutes.
